val build_house : 'a -> 'b -> 'c

build_house b pos is the board b resulting from building a house on the tile at position pos. Requires: pos is from 1–19. Raises OccupiedCorner if the corner already has a road or settlement.

val build_road : 'a -> 'b -> 'c

build_road b pos is the board b resulting from building a road on the tile at position pos. Requires: pos is from 1–19.
